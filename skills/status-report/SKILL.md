When asked for a status update or when running periodically via Hermes cron, gather:
1. **Tasks completed**: recent coding, scaffolding, and verification milestones.
2. **Tasks remaining**: pending features, tests, or deployments.
3. **Decisions needed**: human approvals or feedback required.

Reply in exactly three sections:
**What I Did** / **What's Left** / **What Needs Your Call**.
